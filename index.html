    GREEN DIWALI


    Green or eco-friendly Diwali is the same as the festival of lights we celebrate every year, but the difference is that we also ensure that the environment isn't harmfully impacted. A green Diwali is a festival in which we avoid carelessness and behave like responsible citizens to protect our environment.


    SAY NO TO CRACKERS


    Burning crackers plays a major role in creating air pollution. Crackers not only produce noise but pollute the air as well which leads to respiratory problems. People with breathing diseases are prone to such problems as their immune is easily targeted.


    INJURIES CAUSED


    Most of the victims sustained eye injuries while bursting crackers. At least 78 cases of eye injuries were reported from Sarojini Devi Eye Hospital and LV Prasad Eye Institute. SD Eye Hospital alone saw 41 cases since Diwali night, which was nearly double the tally at the hospital from 2021 when 24 cases were reported.
